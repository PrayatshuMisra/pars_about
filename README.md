PARS is an AI-powered healthcare operations platform built to reduce administrative burden on hospitals and improve patient care delivery. It operates across two connected product lines:
1. Emergency Department Triage
AI analyzes patient symptoms, vitals, and medical history to help clinical staff assess risk levels and route patients appropriately — acting as a decision-support layer, not a replacement for clinicians. Transparent, explainable recommendations are a key design principle.

2. Medical Coding & Clinical Documentation
Automates the conversion of clinical notes into standardized billing and coding formats, reducing manual effort, improving accuracy, cutting claim rejections, and accelerating reimbursements.
The differentiator is that both products live on a single connected platform — data flows continuously from patient intake through triage all the way to the final insurance claim, making it an end-to-end healthcare operations pipeline.

Long-term vision: Evolve into a comprehensive healthcare intelligence platform spanning clinical decision support, workflow automation, documentation intelligence, and operational analytics — helping hospitals improve outcomes while reducing financial inefficiencies.
PARS brings together the clinical and administrative sides of a hospital into one intelligent, automated system.
